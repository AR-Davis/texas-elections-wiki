Leadership

Speaker Election 2025

Dustin Burrows (R) elected January 14, 2025, defeating David Cook (R) 85-55 on second ballot. Burrows received 49 Democratic votes and 36 Republican votes. Former Speaker Dade Phelan did not seek a third term.
