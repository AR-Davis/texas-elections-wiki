About TexasElections.Wiki

Nonpartisan election research and civic data platform

Project Mission

This wiki serves as a centralized, nonpartisan resource for Texas election information—combining official data with research citations to provide accurate, accessible civic knowledge.

Data Sources

  • Texas Legislature Online: Bill status, committee information, voting records
  • Texas Secretary of State: Election results, voting systems certification
  • County Election Officials: Polling locations, early voting procedures
  • Ballotpedia: Candidate information, election calendar
  • Legislative Reference Library: Historical election data, membership statistics

Research Standards

Citation Policy

All factual claims include inline citations to primary sources. Research documents are versioned with compilation dates. Legal analysis references specific code sections and case citations.

Contributing

This is an open research project. Contributions via pull request welcome:

  • Corrections to election data
  • Documented research on contested races
  • Legislative tracking updates
  • County-specific election procedures
